Autumn in Korea

In Korea, autumn falls in the months of September, October, and November. Offering clear cool and crisp weather and the beautiful autumn foliage, autumn is the best time to visit Korea. The best times to see the autumn leaves in Korea are October and November, but the peak season only lasts up to two weeks.
